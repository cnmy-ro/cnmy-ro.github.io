Interactive logical graph of the *"Ethica Ordine Geometrico Demonstrata"* of Benedict de Spinoza. The graph reflects Spinoza's derivation of the propositions in the "Demonstration" sections of his text. Full project available [here](https://github.com/cnmy-ro/ethica).
